About the Institute

Pioneering education for the modern security landscape.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier industry-led training and deployment institute. We are dedicated to providing cutting-edge education in the fields of digital risk, cyber risk, and technology risk to build a resilient future.

By bringing together academic rigor through university partnerships and practical experience via real-world industry projects, we ensure our professionals are ready to tackle tomorrow's security challenges from day one.
